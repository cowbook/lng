## Overview

Phase I of the project includes two 160,000 m3 full containment LNG storage tanks, one jetty capable of berthing 8,000–175,000 m3 LNG carriers, and an annual handling capacity of 2.8 million tonnes (equivalent to 4 billion cubic meters of natural gas). The project also includes a 41.5 km outbound pipeline connecting to the Guangdong provincial pipeline network. The terminal integrates LNG unloading and storage, gas send-out, truck loading, reloading, and emergency peak shaving, offering strong operational flexibility.
